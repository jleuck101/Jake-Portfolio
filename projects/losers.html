LOSERS (Work in Progress)

LOSERS is a Slacker-Fantasy short film with a puppeted dragon. I was brought on the project to help bring the puppet to life, by painting out the puppeteer in a handful of shots. One shot contains about all the challenges you can have, shaky cam, motion blur, human and heavily motion-blurred object occlusion, and the flashing lights. So, I hope to update this page with that challenge completed soon. Glowing and cracked open egg effect also coming soon.
