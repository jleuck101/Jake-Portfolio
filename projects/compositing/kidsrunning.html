Kids Running - Projection Exercise

Compositing - Nuke - Exercise

This exercise involved taking a plate of a street and beautifying it by mirroring the more visually interesting building on the right. Accomplishing this required an understanding that in order to matchmove the mirrored plate correctly, it would first need to be stabilized. This was achieved by frameholding the tracked camera rather than the projected plate, which was then cut out, mirrored, and tracked back in. Additional color corrections and enhancements were made, such as the addition of volume rays.

Key Work

  • Solved the moving plate, stabilized the setup, and used projection workflows to rebuild and extend the environment.
  • Repaired and mirrored the building areas while keeping the reconstruction stable through the camera move.
  • Used holdouts and roto to preserve foreground details cleanly over the rebuilt environment.
  • Finished with sky work, atmosphere, and image balancing so the extension stayed cohesive in motion.
