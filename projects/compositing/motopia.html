Motopia

Compositing - Nuke - CG - Maya

Overview

Built a CG composite in Maya and Nuke for a retrofuturistic "World of Tomorrow" style sequence, centered on designing and integrating the portal effect into the plate. The work focused on multipass CG compositing, portal interaction, distortion, shadow shaping, and environment support so the effect felt grounded in the shot rather than layered on top of it.

What I Did

  • Built the portal effect and integrated it into the live-action plate.
  • Rebuilt Arnold AOVs for the portals, car, and floating island.
  • Used camera tracking and isolation work to anchor the CG to the shot.
  • Added portal-driven distortion, shadow shaping, and lightwrap interaction.
  • Developed supporting details including tail-light streaks, sign paint out, and tunnel/background support.
