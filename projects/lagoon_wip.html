The Lagoon (Work in Progress)

Compositing • Nuke

This is the big one. By far the largest scale I've worked on on a short film. Responsible for over a dozen shots, heavily featuring the "face splitting" effect. Developed pipeline for the effect and implemented it across a multitude of shots, lighting environments, and story beats to help sell the idea of the character both "splitting" and "becoming whole" in different shots. Large technical challenges in each different environment that have sharpened my skills greatly. Solutions and tools developed for each scenario. Even more so, the challenge of telling the story within my shots, as I have "pieces" of both sides of her character at war with each other within my shots, and must animate, sync to VA, and succinctly fit that internal battle into my shots, and have it play out across sequences.

More in-depth breakdown + additional shots are on their way.
