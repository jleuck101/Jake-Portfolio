Portfolio Summary:
Executed a large-scale 4K paint cleanup on a wide static lakeside pavilion shot, removing multiple background distractions and rebuilding architectural and natural elements across the frame. Created clean patches from scratch, reconstructed tree detail from multiple source areas, rebuilt sections visible through the arches, and cleaned up cars and other unwanted elements in the background. Integrated the work with manual roto and holdouts, frame holds, time offsets, denoise, and final regrain.

What I Did:
- Removed cars and other background distractions visible through the pavilion arches
- Created clean patches from scratch
- Rebuilt tree areas using pieces sourced from different parts of the plate
- Painted and patched portions of the arches using information from elsewhere in the shot
- Used manual roto and holdouts for trees, lamps, and overlapping architectural areas
- Keyed moving foreground grass to help cover or isolate areas during cleanup
- Used frame holds and time offsets to find workable background tree motion
- Handled denoise and regrain integration for the final comp
- Managed slight plate drift in an otherwise static shot
