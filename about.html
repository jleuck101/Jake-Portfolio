Hey! I'm Jake, a Nuke compositor and recent DePaul VFX graduate, with minors in animation and computer science.

I'm an avid problem solver. When something isn't working perfectly, I can't help myself from wanting to understand every part of it. I have found Visual Effects to be the perfect field to scratch that itch.

I started making YouTube content in 2021 and grew from shorts editing to higher-effort compositing work in Resolve/Fusion. Since getting hands-on with Nuke at DePaul, I've loved how you can break things down to the smallest atom. There's definitely a nuke pun in there somewhere. That granularity and precision has made working with all digital images more clear, and turned Nuke into my own pixel sandbox. I love digging in that sandbox to find the truth of the image, how it would actually look. I also very much enjoy turning shovels into excavators, creating tools that let myself and my teams get to the artistry part quicker. All for the ultimate goal of creating something beautiful that suspends the disbelief of the viewer, doing my part in bringing the world of a story to life.
