About

Nuke compositing + pipeline-minded problem solving.

Hey!

I'm Jake, a Nuke compositor and recent DePaul VFX graduate, with minors in animation and computer science.

I'm an avid problem solver. When something isn't working perfectly, I can't help myself from wanting to understand every part of it. I have found Visual Effects to be the perfect field to scratch that itch.

I started making YouTube content in 2021 and grew from shorts editing to higher-effort compositing work in Resolve/Fusion. Since getting hands-on with Nuke at DePaul, I've loved the clarity and precision it gives for manipulating imagery and building shots logically. I care about both quality and efficiency. Making things as cool as I can, but making it repeatable. Keeping my work readable, my workflow standardized, and building whatever tool that might smooth out the next bump in the road.
